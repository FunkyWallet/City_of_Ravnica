Embracing the Natural Order

The Golgari Sanitation Department is not your typical waste management company. Nestled deep within the intricate layers of Ravnica's sprawling metropolis, we are a guild rooted in a profound understanding of life's cyclical nature. Our story begins where others shy away - in the realms of decay, transformation, and reneward.

Guardians of Ravnica's Sustainability

In the heart of this vast urban expanse, we have undertaken a solemn duty: the stewardship of waste and the cultivation of life from its remnants. Our commitment is to the very essence of Ravnica itself. With unwavering dedication, we ensure the city remains clean, resilient, and sustainable.

The Art of Waste Transformation

Our unique approach to waste management harnesses the ancient arts of necromancy and bio-engineering. Through these arcane practices, we turn the discarded and deceased into something remarkable. What others see as waste, we view as a resource, breathing new life into Ravnica's soil and ecosystems.

A Guild Like No Other

The Golgari Swarm stands apart, shunning the pursuit of power that consumes many other guilds. Instead, we embrace the rhythms of nature, forging a harmonious coexistence with the world beneath the city streets. Our commitment to sustainability extends beyond waste management; it is a way of life.

Join Us on the Journey

We invite you to explore the depths of our unique approach to sanitation, where the boundaries between life and death blur. Discover how we breathe life into the discarded and revitalize Ravnica's ecosystems. The Golgari Sanitation Department welcomes you to be a part of this extraordinary journey -- where death meets renewal, and waste is transformed into a thriving future.

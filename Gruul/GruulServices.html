Wilderness Survival Workshops

Join our seasoned Gruul warriors

Our immersive wilderness survival workshops teach essential skills such as building shelters, starting fires, and foraging for food. Discover the art of thriving in the wild, honing your primal instincts, and embracing the untamed side of Ravnica.

Raging River Expeditions

Adrenaline Fueled Adventure!

Seek adrenaline-fueled adventure on our Raging River Rafting Expeditions. Navigate the city's untamed waterways, guided by experienced Gruul guides. Conquer challenging rapids, discover hidden grottoes, and experience the breathtaking beauty of Ravnica's wilderness from the heart of the raging river.

Gathering Festival

Join Us!

These lively gatherings celebrate the natural world with bonfires, drum circles, and storytelling under the starlit sky. Feast on delicious, foraged cuisine and connect with fellow citizens as you embrace the wild side of the city. These festivals are a testament to the Gruul's enduring bond with the untamed, where every heart beats in rhythm with the primal beat of Ravnica.
